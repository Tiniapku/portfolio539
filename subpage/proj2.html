Ufind

Ufind (see here) is a part of team project in international summer course Web Product Design holded by The Department of Information Management and School of Information of University of Washington, Seattle. In this course, we spent 3 weeks to design a mobile application under instruction. The 2014 summer course's topic assigned was "Online to Offline".

It was great fun to go through the whole process of brainstorming, interviewing, prototyping, designing and persuading our intended audience or "investor" to support our idea. We worked in a team of 8, students were from the United States, India, Korean and China. We all became good friends after that.

Actually it was the first website I made, and it was totally independent. I taught myself HTML and CSS and some javascript to develop this. Now looking back I find it is so childish and ugly, but I still like it, as well as the fantastic 3 weeks.

The website was on the summer course's server so I cannot improve it. I really hope I can do something!
