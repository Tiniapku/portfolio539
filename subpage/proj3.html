Summit IDG

Summit IDG (see here) is an Accessibility Challenge project we heard from SI 539 Web Development In this project, teams are assigned a non-profit organization to improve the accessibility of their website. Summit IDG is our client.

Summit IDG is the short for Summit International Development Group. The orgranization aims at challenging, supporting, aiding and honoring Wounded Warrios and First Responders as they set, meet and exceed their fitness goals. We are now working on this project. So I cannot link our work to my portfolio now.

We have a group of 6 student and we are all from UMSI. This is an amazing team! I've learnt a lot from every team member. We are very different but we coordinate very well. Everyone is passionate, diligent, and forthcoming. Our mentor Kara is also helpful and insightful.

I am very happy to have this chance. It is great to have hand-on experience on a realife website and make it better for realife audiences. Thank you SI 539, thank you Colleen.
