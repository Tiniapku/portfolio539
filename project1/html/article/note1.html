花香调

东方花香调(Floral Oriental)

Soft, spicy orange flower notes meld with piquant aldehydes and sweet spices to create the heart of a Floral Oriental fragrance.Born in the 1900s, Floral Orientals came back to life again in the 1970s. In the past decade, lively, fruity interpretations dominated the Floral Oriental category, but recent fragrances have developed a more subtle, muted personality.

轻柔的，带有柑橘的辛辣气息，混合清爽的醛类和甜香香料共同组成了东方花香调。

淡花香调(Soft Floral)

The marriage of sparkling aldehydes and delicate flowers creates a family of soft, often powdery, abstract florals.Aldehydes are found naturally in rose and citrus oils, but in such minute amounts that they have to be re-created in the laboratory. Their natural scent is not pleasant: some have a sharp, metallic fragrance, others the burnt, waxy aroma of a justsnuffed candle. Add them to flowers, however, and their subtle magic makes the blossoms sing. Their soprano notes are muted by the powdery accents of iris and vanilla to create a fragrance that is both soft and flowery.

活泼的醛类香气和精致的画像共同组成了轻柔细腻的、抽象的淡花香调。醛类香料一般在天然产物如玫瑰和橡树油中提取，但是由于其原本的气味很刺激，真正应用之前，是需要在实验室里重制的。

花香调(Floral)

Florals remain the most popular fragrance family.Their repertoire is vast, ranging from concertos on the theme of a single floral note to mighty symphonies of heady mixed bouquets. Headspace technology has given perfumers an avalanche of exciting new floral notes: it allows them to identify and clone the scent of blooms from which no oil can be extracted by traditional methods. Each year, unusual new notes are found, revitalising the traditional floral theme.

花香调在香水家族里是最普遍的。 他们的特点是广泛，既可以是单种花香调（类比协奏曲），也可以是多种混合花香（类比交响乐）。

果香调

Peaches and pears, apples and plums. A twist of tropical fruits.Essences of strawberry, raspberry and berries of all hues. Add a splash of flowers to create a family of fruity cocktails that smell delicious.

蜜桃和雪梨、苹果和甜李、以及一些热带水果的组合、草莓、山莓各种莓都是果香调香水的宠儿。在果香调中点入花香就如同新制的鸡尾酒，让人垂涎欲滴。
